üéØ Our Mission

Vaani aims to break down communication barriers for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community by providing real-time AI-powered captioning and sign language assistance. We believe that everyone deserves equal access to information and communication, regardless of their hearing abilities.

Our mission is to create an inclusive digital environment where deaf individuals can participate fully in conversations, education, and professional settings. Through cutting-edge AI technology, we're making communication accessible to millions of people across India and beyond.

üí° What is Vaani?

Vaani (meaning "voice" in Hindi) is an intelligent captioning system that converts spoken language into deaf-friendly text and sign language interpretations. Powered by Google's advanced AI technologies, Vaani provides accurate, real-time transcription and simplification in multiple Indian languages.

The platform uses sophisticated natural language processing to transform complex spoken sentences into clear, easy-to-understand text. This ensures that deaf users can follow conversations in real-time without missing critical information.

Beyond simple transcription, Vaani intelligently simplifies vocabulary and sentence structure while preserving the original meaning and emotional context of the speech.

üöÄ Technology Stack

Our platform is built on enterprise-grade Google Cloud technologies:

  • Google Gemini AI: Advanced language processing and text simplification with context-aware understanding
  • Speech-to-Text API: Real-time audio transcription with support for multiple Indian languages and accents
  • Cloud Firestore: Secure, scalable NoSQL database for storing transcripts and user preferences
  • Firebase Storage: Media asset management for sign language video content
  • FastAPI Backend: High-performance Python API server with async processing capabilities
  • WebSocket Technology: Real-time bidirectional communication for instant caption delivery
